# AI Ticket Platform

## One-Line Description

An AI-powered customer support automation platform that combines Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG), semantic search, and ticket analytics to streamline support operations.

## What Problem Does It Solve?

Support teams spend significant time manually categorizing, routing, and responding to customer tickets.

This platform automates ticket analysis, retrieves relevant knowledge base information, and provides support teams with AI-assisted workflows.

## Target Users

- SaaS Companies
- Customer Support Teams
- Internal IT Helpdesks
- Small Businesses
- Enterprise Support Operations

## Core Value Proposition

Reduce support workload while improving response quality through AI-powered knowledge retrieval and ticket processing.
